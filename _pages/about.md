Hi! I'm Bhargava, and my research interests lie at the intersection of generative modeling and reinforcement learning for general-purpose robotics applications. I'm currently a Research Assistant at [Mila](https://mila.quebec/en) advised by [Prof. Glen Berseth](https://neo-x.github.io/), where I currently work on using foundation models to scale data for training generalist robot policies.

Prior to this, I worked with LLMs/VLMs in industry for a couple of years. In a past life, I was an undergrad in Electronics engineering at [BITS Pilani](https://www.bits-pilani.ac.in/) Hyderabad Campus. Here, I had the opportunity to collaborate with [Prof. Pratyush Chakraborty](https://www.linkedin.com/in/pratyush-chakraborty-ph-d-abb7982b/) and [Prof. Paresh Saxena](https://in.linkedin.com/in/saxena-paresh) in reinforcement learning, curriculum learning, and autonomous driving research.

Feel free to contact me by email to chat anything research!
